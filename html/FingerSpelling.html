Finger Spelling
Use the chart to the left to learn to sign the letters of the alphabet. Finger spelling is used to sign names or words that there is no sign for.
  1. With your left or right hand near your shoulder, sign the first letter.
  2. Slide your hand over and sign the next letter. If you are using your left hand slide your hand to the left. If you are using your right hand, slide your hand to the right.
  3. Continue this process until the name or word your want the spell is complete.
